General

This is a multi zone large office EnergyPlus model. It is based on DOE Commercial Reference Large Office Building model, post-1980 construction, ASHRAE Standards 90.1-1989 and 62-1999. The original model has 12 floors (10 identical middle floors) with a basement. Due to the current limitation of Spawn for the floor multiplier, this emulator only contains three different floors (i.e., top, middle, bottom). The ground floor assumes adiabatic with the basement.

Building Design and Use

Architecture

Number of Stories = 3; Shape = rectangle, Aspect ratio = 1.5.

Constructions

Envelope thermal properties vary with climate according to ASHRAE Standard 90.1-1989. Opaque constructions: mass walls; built-up flat roof (insulation above deck); slab-on-grade floor Windows: window-to-wall ratio = 38.0%, equal distribution of windows Infiltration in perimeter zones only = 1.5 cfm/ft2 above grade wall area at 0.3 in wc (75 Pa) adjusted to 0.016 in wc (4 Pa). 25% of full value when ventilation system on.

Occupancy schedules

See BLDG_OCC_SCH in attached idf.

Internal loads and schedules

See BLDG_LIGHT_SCH, BLDG_EQUIP_SCH, BLDG_ELEVATORS in attached idf.

Climate data

The weather data is from TMY3 for Chicago O'Hare International Airport.

HVAC System Design

The HVAC system are ideal load systems, which calculates the sensible/latent cooling/heating load.
